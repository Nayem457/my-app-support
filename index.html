Welcome to the support center. We are dedicated to providing the best experience for our community. If you can't find your answer below, please reach out to us.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I suggest a new hymn?

You can submit suggestions directly through the app. Please provide the title and lyrics; our team will review the submission, and it will be added to the library as soon as possible.

I found a typo in a hymn. How can I report it?

Accuracy is very important to us. Please email us with the name of the hymn and the correction, or use the "Report" button within the app.

Still need help?

If you're experiencing technical issues or have other questions, send us an email and we'll get back to you quickly.
