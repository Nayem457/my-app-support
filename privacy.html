Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the EOTC Hymns app handles your information.

Information We Collect

The EOTC Hymns app is designed to minimize data collection. We do not sell your personal information and we do not use the app to track your location or access your contacts.

Most users can browse hymns and use core features without creating an account. However, some features such as iCloud sync, shared collections, or purchases may involve Apple services connected to your device or Apple ID.

Local Storage

The app may store content and preferences locally on your device, such as favorites, settings, and other app data, to improve your experience.

iCloud and Sync Features

If you use features such as synced collections, shared collections, or other iCloud-based functionality, related data may be stored securely through Apple’s CloudKit and iCloud services so it can sync across your devices or be shared with others when you choose.

Purchases

If you subscribe to Pro or make in-app purchases, payment processing and subscription management are handled by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your full payment information.

Third-Party Services

The app may rely on Apple services such as StoreKit and CloudKit to support subscriptions, syncing, and sharing features. The app does not use advertising frameworks that track user behavior for ads.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Data is only shared when necessary to provide features you choose to use, such as iCloud sync, shared collections, or Apple-managed subscriptions.

Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
