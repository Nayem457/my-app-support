Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how the EOTC Hymns app handles your data.

Data Collection

The EOTC Hymns app is designed to be fully private. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not track your location, access your contacts, or require a user account to use the app.

Local Storage

All app content, including hymns, favorites, and custom settings, is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your phone and is not accessible by us.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party analytics or advertising frameworks that track user behavior.
